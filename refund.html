Refund Policy

Last updated: 26 March 2026

30-day money-back guarantee. If PAXA Solo is not right for you, email us within 30 days of purchase and we will refund you in full. No forms, no questions, no conditions.

1. Our guarantee

We are confident in the science behind PAXA Solo. But we also know every dog — and every owner — is different. If the workbook is not the right fit for your situation, we do not keep your money.

You have 30 days from the date of purchase to request a full refund, for any reason or no reason at all.

2. How to request a refund

Email hello@paxapet.co.uk with:

  • The email address you used to purchase
  • Your Gumroad order number (found in your purchase confirmation email)

That is all we need. We do not require a reason, proof of use, or any other information.

3. Refund processing

We will process your refund within 2 business days of receiving your request. The refund is issued via Gumroad back to your original payment method. Depending on your bank or card provider, it may take 5–10 business days to appear on your statement.

4. Statutory rights

PAXA Solo is a digital product supplied by immediate download. Under the Consumer Contracts (Information, Cancellation and Additional Charges) Regulations 2013, the statutory 14-day cancellation right for digital content is waived upon download.

Our 30-day guarantee is a voluntary commercial commitment that exceeds your statutory rights. It does not affect any other statutory rights you may have under UK consumer law, including the Consumer Rights Act 2015.

5. After a refund

Upon refund, your licence to use PAXA Solo is revoked. You are asked to delete all copies of the PDF from your devices. We trust our customers to do this in good faith.

6. Eligibility

All purchases of PAXA Solo are eligible for a refund under this policy within the 30-day window. There are no exceptions based on how much of the workbook you have completed.

7. Contact

For any questions about your purchase or this policy, contact us at hello@paxapet.co.uk. We aim to respond within 1 business day.
