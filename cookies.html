Cookie Policy

Last updated: 26 March 2026

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, which cookies paxapet.co.uk uses, and how you can control them. We comply with the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR) and UK GDPR.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, understand how you use the site, and deliver certain functionality. Some cookies are set directly by us; others are set by third-party services we use.

2. What cookies do we use?

We use the following categories of cookies on paxapet.co.uk:

We do not currently use advertising or retargeting cookies. If this changes, we will update this policy.

3. Legal basis for cookies

  • Strictly necessary cookies (e.g., Gumroad session): these are required for basic functionality and do not require your consent under PECR.
  • Analytics and marketing cookies (Google Analytics, Klaviyo): these require your consent. Where consent is required, we will ask for it before placing these cookies.

4. How to control cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Browser settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Refer to your browser's help documentation. Note that blocking certain cookies may affect the functionality of this website.
  • Google Analytics opt-out: Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • Klaviyo: If you are an email subscriber, you can unsubscribe at any time via the link in any email. This will also limit Klaviyo's ability to track your site activity.

5. Third-party cookies

Some cookies on our site are set by third parties (Google, Klaviyo, Gumroad). We do not control these cookies directly. Please refer to the respective third-party privacy policies for more information on how they use data collected via cookies.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
