# PAXA Output Formats — Commercial Templates

## Critical Rule Before Using Any Template

**PAXA content is COMMERCIAL, not informational.**

The goal of every piece of content is one of:
1. Drive traffic to paxapet.co.uk
2. Capture an email address
3. Convert to a £29 purchase

If the content doesn't serve one of these three goals — don't create it.

## 1. TikTok / Instagram Reels Script

**Format:** Hook → Problem agitation → Counter-intuitive insight → Authority proof → CTA

**Length:** 30–45 seconds spoken (roughly 80–120 words)

**Template:**
```
HOOK (0–3 seconds — must stop the scroll):
[Counter-intuitive statement or surprising fact]

PROBLEM (3–10 seconds):
[Name the specific pain point the viewer is experiencing right now]

INSIGHT (10–25 seconds):
[The counter-intuitive truth — what they're doing wrong and why]
[One specific, science-backed reason why PAXA works differently]

PROOF (25–35 seconds):
[One concrete result or fact — no generic claims]

CTA (35–45 seconds):
"Link in bio — get Days 1 and 2 free. No card needed."
```

**Example:**
```
HOOK:
"If you comfort your dog before you leave — you're making their anxiety worse."

PROBLEM:
"Most owners spend 20 minutes saying goodbye. Hugs, treats, 'be a good boy.' 
Then they wonder why their dog destroys the sofa the moment the door closes."

INSIGHT:
"The goodbye ritual is the problem. Your dog has learned it predicts your absence. 
The more emotional it is, the more certain they become that something bad is coming.
The protocol says: leave like it's nothing. Because it should be nothing."

PROOF:
"PAXA Solo is 30 days of systematic desensitisation. 
The same science behaviourists charge £80 an hour for. £29. Instant download."

CTA:
"Link in bio. Get Days 1 and 2 free — no card needed."
```

## 4. Reddit Post (Community Seeding — Subtle Commercial)

**Platform:** r/dogs, r/puppy101, r/DogAdvice, r/Dogtraining

**Rules:**
- Never mention PAXA by name in the post body
- Provide genuine value — answer the question properly
- Drop the link only in a comment, not the post
- Never say "I work for" or "I made" — write as a dog owner

**Template:**
```
TITLE:
[Genuine question or experience headline — not salesy]
Example: "Finally figured out why my cockapoo's anxiety got worse when I tried to 'comfort' him"

BODY:
[Personal experience framing — write as the owner, not the brand]
[The counter-intuitive insight you want to share]
[Specific detail that shows expertise without being promotional]
[Open question to the community]

COMMENT (post 10–15 mins after):
"Someone DM'd me asking what protocol I followed — it was this one: [link]
Days 1 and 2 are free if you want to try it before committing."
```

## 6. Email Subject Lines — Nurture Sequence Style

**Rules:**
- No emojis in subject line for cold/warm sequences (exception: one emoji max for existing list)
- Never use "FREE" in caps in subject lines (spam trigger)
- Always write for curiosity or counter-intuition — not benefit

**Format options:**
```
Counter-intuitive:
"The one thing you should never do when you leave your dog"

Curiosity gap:
"What your dog does in the first 4 minutes after you leave"

Direct challenge:
"Calming treats don't work. Here's what does."

Personal/empathetic:
"You didn't cause this. But you can fix it."

Specificity:
"Day 1: Find your dog's anxiety threshold (takes 20 minutes)"
```

## 7. SEO Blog Post Structure (Commercial Intent)

**This is not a neutral educational post. Every blog post sells.**

```
TITLE (H1):
[Keyword-led but counter-intuitive]
Example: "Why Calming Treats Make Dog Separation Anxiety Worse"

META DESCRIPTION (150 chars):
[Pain point + differentiator + implicit CTA]

INTRO (150 words max):
[Agitate the problem they came to solve]
[Promise the counter-intuitive answer]
[DO NOT bury the lead]

SECTION 1 — THE PROBLEM (H2):
[Why the conventional approach fails — neurological explanation]

SECTION 2 — THE COUNTER-INTUITIVE TRUTH (H2):
[What actually works — introduce the systematic approach]

SECTION 3 — HOW THE PROTOCOL WORKS (H2):
[Brief overview of PAXA's 4-phase system — not a full explanation]
[Link to paxapet.co.uk]

SECTION 4 — WHAT TO EXPECT (H2):
[Timeline, realistic outcomes, honest caveats]

CTA BLOCK (before final section):
[Inline CTA — free lead magnet or direct purchase]

CLOSING (100 words max):
[Reinforce the core reframe]
[Final CTA]
```

[Specific supporting detail — one sentence]

[Never more than 3 sentences per paragraph]

[Action verb + product name + price]
Example: "Start the Protocol — £29 →"

MICROCOPY BELOW CTA:

Example: "30-day money back · No questions · Instant download"

## Output Quality Checklist

Before delivering any output, verify:

- [ ] UK English spelling throughout
- [ ] Zero exclamation marks in product/brand copy
- [ ] CTA included with correct link
- [ ] No generic pet brand language ("fur baby", "pawesome")
- [ ] Counter-intuitive angle present (not just benefit-led)
- [ ] Science term used correctly (if used)
- [ ] Length appropriate for format
- [ ] Commercial purpose clear — not just educational
